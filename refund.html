Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: [DATE]

Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from within the Service, using the "Manage / cancel subscription" option. Cancellation takes effect immediately — your next billing cycle will not be charged, and Pro features (SMS and WhatsApp alerts) stop right away. Your account reverts to the Free plan (email alerts continue).

Refunds

Because cancellation is immediate and Pro is billed monthly in advance, we do not provide prorated refunds for the unused portion of a billing period you've already paid for.

If you were charged in error — for example, a duplicate charge or a charge after you had already cancelled — contact us at [your-support-email@example.com] and we'll investigate and issue a refund for confirmed billing errors. Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method via Razorpay, typically within 5–7 business days.

Failed payments

If a renewal payment fails, we'll flag your account but won't immediately downgrade you — Razorpay may retry the charge. If payment continues to fail, your account will move to the Free plan.

Contact

Billing questions or refund requests: see our Contact Us page.
