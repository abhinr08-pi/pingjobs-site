This Privacy Policy explains what information pingjobs ("we", "us", "our") collects, how we use it, and who we share it with.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact details: email address, and phone number if you enable SMS or WhatsApp alerts.
  • Preferences: the keywords you set for matching, and which alert channels you've chosen.
  • Billing information: if you upgrade to Pro, our payment processor Razorpay collects and processes your payment details. We do not receive or store your card, UPI, or bank details ourselves.

2. How we use your information

  • To match new listings against your keywords and deliver alerts through your chosen channel(s).
  • To process Pro subscription payments and manage billing status.
  • To respond if you contact us with a question or issue.

3. Who we share information with

We share the minimum necessary data with these third parties to operate the Service:

  • Razorpay — payment processing for Pro subscriptions.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) — hosting, storage, and sending of email/SMS alerts.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data retention

We retain your subscriber information for as long as your account is active. If you unsubscribe, we mark your record inactive and stop sending alerts; you may request full deletion by contacting us (see below).

5. Your rights

You can update your keyword preferences or alert channels at any time, unsubscribe from alerts via the link in every email, or cancel your Pro subscription from within the Service. To request a copy or deletion of your data, contact us using the details below.

6. Security

We use industry-standard practices (encrypted connections, access controls on our cloud infrastructure) to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
